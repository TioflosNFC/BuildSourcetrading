About Us

A local supplier built around fast, dependable finishing materials

Build Source Trading supplies construction and finishing materials to contractors, developers, and individual builders across Addis Ababa. We work directly with manufacturers and distributors so our customers get consistent quality at fair prices — without the delays that slow down a site.

From gypsum ceilings to soundproofing and waterproofing systems, we stock the categories that finishing work actually depends on, and we keep communication simple: call, WhatsApp, or Telegram — whatever's fastest for you.

  • Mission — Make quality finishing materials accessible and affordable for every builder in Addis Ababa.
  • Vision — Become the most trusted finishing-materials supplier in Ethiopia's construction sector.
  • Why choose us — Direct sourcing, fast delivery, and a team that answers the phone.
